Tutoring

All handouts

    Starting week 2, we have small group tutoring every week (Saturday through Friday). At each tutoring session, we provide a handout reviewing the previous week's topics

    Make the most out of tutoring! Each session has at most 6 students, so tutors can give you more personalized help. This is your chance to ask any specific questions about the material and solidify your understanding.

    Sign ups

    You can sign up for tutoring here. To sign up for a tutoring session, click on a slot and click save.

    Please only sign up for one tutoring session at a time. All tutoring sessions in the same week (Saturday to Friday) cover the same material.
